ABOUT ME

As a young and inspired artist and designer, I'm currently completing my undergraduate degree at the University of Waterloo. I'm enrolled as an Arts and Business student specializing in digital arts. While I complete this degree I'm currently learning digital design concepts that allow me to prepare for upcoming and current opportunities. In addition to my interest in design, I have a love for digital and analog photography, as well as music, fashion, and literature. Going forward I'm excited to explore different industries where design is applicable. I'm hoping to learn more about UX/UI design, graphic design, and marketing design.
